# JomBoss Weekly PA Workflow

Source: `00_SYSTEM/00_SOURCE_PDFS/WEEKLY_PA_WORKFLOW_EXTRACT.md`

## Cadence

- Frequency: weekly
- Preferred timing: Sunday night or Monday morning
- Duration: 45 to 90 minutes
- Owner: G
- Assistant: Codex

## Weekly Review Prompt

Run the JomBoss Weekly PA Review.

Read these files first:

1. `00_SYSTEM/JOMBOSS_MASTER_PROTOCOL.md`
2. `00_SYSTEM/DATA_ROOM_MASTER_CHECKLIST.md`
3. `00_SYSTEM/WEEKLY_PA_WORKFLOW.md`
4. `00_SYSTEM/DECISION_LOG.md`
5. `00_SYSTEM/OPEN_ISSUES.md`
6. `01_COMPANY/CURRENT_TRUTH.md`
7. `10_INVESTOR_DATA_ROOM/DATA_ROOM_GAP_TRACKER.md`

Then generate a CEO Weekly Review covering:

1. What changed this week
2. What documents were added or updated
3. What critical data room items are still missing
4. What claims remain unsupported
5. What financial, legal, or IP risks need attention
6. What client, revenue, or talent updates need recording
7. What decisions require G's approval
8. What should be updated in `CURRENT_TRUTH.md`
9. Top 5 priorities for next week
10. Files Codex should create or update now

Do not invent facts. If information is missing, mark it as missing and propose the next action.

## CEO Priority Logic

Prioritise:

1. Legal ownership or equity
2. IP ownership
3. Revenue verification
4. Client contracts
5. Talent or worker compliance
6. Investor or accelerator diligence
7. Product roadmap clarity
8. Team execution
9. Branding or external presentation
10. Optional improvements
