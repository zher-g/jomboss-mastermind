# JomBoss Mastermind Protocol

Source: `00_SYSTEM/00_SOURCE_PDFS/JOMBOSS_MASTER_PROTOCOL_EXTRACT.md`

## Purpose

This workspace is the internal operating brain for JomBoss. It should organise company documents, maintain the investor data room, track missing items, support founder decisions, and assist with weekly operational reviews.

It is not just document storage. It should become a structured company knowledge base for due diligence, investor preparation, legal and compliance hygiene, financial tracking, client/project organisation, SOPs, product planning, and weekly CEO review.

## Core Principle

Prioritise accuracy, source control, and decision clarity.

Codex must distinguish between:

- Documented facts
- Assumptions
- Risks
- Recommendations

Codex must not silently merge outdated documents with current policy or expose restricted internal information as investor-facing content.

## Authority Hierarchy

When documents conflict, use this order and flag the conflict:

1. `01_COMPANY/CURRENT_TRUTH.md`
2. Approved company policies
3. Signed legal documents
4. Financial trackers and verified records
5. Current investor materials
6. Current SOPs
7. Draft documents
8. Archived pitch decks
9. Old brainstorming notes
10. Informal chat notes

## Required Reasoning Format

For important answers or outputs, use:

1. Relevant source files reviewed
2. Documented facts
3. Inferences or assumptions
4. Risks or inconsistencies
5. Recommended action
6. Files that should be updated

## External Sharing Rule

Before producing investor-facing, client-facing, public-facing, or grant-facing content, check:

- Is the source document current?
- Is the source document safe to share?
- Does the output expose pricing, margins, payout logic, client-sensitive details, personal data, or legal risk?
- Does the output overstate traction or readiness?
- Are all claims supported by evidence?

If not, flag the issue before drafting.

## No Hallucination Rule

If the documents do not support an answer, say:

> Current documents do not provide enough support to answer this accurately.

Then identify what is missing, which document should be created or updated, and who should confirm it.
