- IP ownership
- Client contract terms
- Talent payout rules
- PM model
- Product roadmap
- Fundraising strategy
- Investor commitments
- Grant or accelerator obligations
- Legal or compliance assumptions
- Strategic direction

## Decision: Build JomBoss Mastermind Codex Repository

**Date:** 2026-06-18

**Status:** Approved

**Decision Owner:** G

**Approved By:** G

**Related Files:**

- `00_SYSTEM/JOMBOSS_MASTER_PROTOCOL.md`
- `00_SYSTEM/DATA_ROOM_MASTER_CHECKLIST.md`
- `00_SYSTEM/WEEKLY_PA_WORKFLOW.md`
- `01_COMPANY/CURRENT_TRUTH.md`

### Decision Made

JomBoss will build a Codex-based internal knowledge repository called the JomBoss Mastermind.

The system will be used to organise the company data room, maintain source-of-truth documents, assist with weekly CEO review, and support future automation.

### Reason

JomBoss has reached a stage where company information is spread across pitch decks, SOPs, contracts, financial records, client documents, and informal conversations.

This creates risk because:

- Investors may ask for due diligence documents
- Old information may conflict with current company truth
- Founder and team roles may appear inconsistent
- Revenue claims need supporting evidence
- IP ownership needs to be organised
- Legal and compliance gaps need tracking
- The company needs a repeatable system before scaling

### Alternatives Considered

1. Continue using Google Drive without structured AI support
2. Build only a normal investor data room
3. Use Notion as the main company knowledge base
4. Build a Codex repository with Markdown source-of-truth files

### Expected Impact

This should improve:

- Data room readiness
- Investor credibility
- Internal organisation
- Weekly task tracking
- Legal and compliance awareness
- Founder decision-making
- Platform and SOP development

### Risks

- If documents are not classified properly, sensitive information may be exposed
- If outdated documents are not archived, Codex may produce conflicting outputs
- If updates are not done weekly, the repository may become stale
- If source-of-truth files are incomplete, Codex may give weak recommendations
