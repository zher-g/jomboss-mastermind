## 1. Purpose

This document defines the weekly operating rhythm for the JomBoss Codex PA system.
The goal is to make sure JomBoss does not lose track of documents, decisions, clients, revenue, legal risks,
product progress, or investor-readiness tasks.
The weekly PA workflow should help G act like the CEO of an organised company, not a founder relying on
memory.

Recommended review frequency:
Once per week
Preferably Sunday night or Monday morning
Duration: 45 to 90 minutes
Owner: G
Assistant: Codex

## 6. Weekly Data Room Maintenance

Each week, Codex should check:
Are any critical documents still missing?
Are any draft documents now ready?
Are any old documents still being treated as current?
Are any files missing classification labels?
Are any investor-facing claims unsupported?
Are any legal or financial files stored in the wrong location?
Are restricted internal documents accidentally placed in investor-safe folders?
Are new client or revenue records properly linked to evidence?
Are new decisions recorded in DECISION_LOG.md?
Are unresolved issues recorded in OPEN_ISSUES.md?
